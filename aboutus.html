About Ace :

What are we ?

Academy Ace is the first online P2P university tutoring platform in Montreal: we provide a long range of available classes that can be taught by the community. Just create an account, and you will get the possibility to make money by becoming a tutor, or to ace your exams by finding the perfect learning solution. Tutors are rated by students, allowing a convenient and fluid market, at unbeatable prices! Join the family now!

Our goal :

Ace aim to provide affordable tutoring solutions for every student and increase the number of classes available for left aside degrees. We believe that nobody understands students better than students: come share your knowledge and thirst for success, and Ace your academic future.

Why Ace ?

We want to provide a large range of services to help out the universities communities, as tools, documents and of course tutoring. With us, you get the best of the student state of mind at affordable prices, as we don’t take any fees on the payments between student and tutors: we simply are a checkpoint on your path to success!

Who are we

Academy Ace is a Canadian startup completely composed of Concordia students, who believe in mutual aid between human’s beings. Be sure to check those pretty faces!
