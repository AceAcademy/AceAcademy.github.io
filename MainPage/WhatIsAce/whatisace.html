What is Ace ?

A wide P2P tutoring platform

Any students who have taken a class previously can apply to become a tutor.
This brings more tutors on the market, thus more classes offered.




Adaptive and Flexible

Tutors model their own session (date, time, content, method).
Students pick the criteria that match with their expectations.




Verified tutor profiles

Students who had a final grade higher than A- can apply to be certified.
A tutor will be certified once his transcript is uploaded.




Market rate prices and hassle free payment

A tutor can choose its own pricing for a session.
Once decided, the student pays online to Ace, and the amount is only transferred to tutor when both tutor and student submit their tutoring session minutes.
